NLPearl.AI offers the flexibility to purchase phone numbers from various countries directly through our platform. Currently, US phone numbers are available for immediate purchase. For phone numbers from other countries, please contact our support team.

### Available Countries

At present, you can directly purchase phone numbers from the following country:

- **United States**

### Contact Support for Other Countries

For phone numbers from countries other than the USA, you can contact our support team. Due to specific regulations in many countries, clients may need to provide business or personal identification documents. This process ensures compliance with local laws and may take a few days for approval.

### Regulations and Documentation

When purchasing a phone number from certain countries, the following documentation may be required:

- **Business Identification**: Proof of business registration and related documents.
- **Personal Identification**: Government-issued ID or passport.

Our support team will guide you through the necessary steps and document requirements for your specific country.

### Twilio Integration

If you already have specific phone numbers from Twilio that you want to use with NLPearl.AI, you can integrate them by entering your Twilio credentials. This allows you to leverage your existing Twilio numbers with our Pearl conversational flows.
